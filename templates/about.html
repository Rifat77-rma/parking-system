About Our Services

Booking Procedure

For booking a slot ahead of time, reach out to us via Email or Contact us through our hotline. (Check ‘Contact Us’ for details).

For instant booking, show up with your vehicle, and our dedicated employees will be there to assist you.

Necessary Information for booking

  • Name
  • Contact no.
  • Emergency contact
  • National ID number
  • Address

Payment Details

Standard Booking: $20 per hour.

Extended Booking: $15 per additional hour.

Payment for Damages: Costs vary based on the extent of the damage.

Late Fees: $5 for every additional 15 minutes beyond the booked time.
